Help

Welcome to Accessible Sudoku, to start, please read the instructions carefully before you begin to play.

  • To navigate around the game, you can use the arrow keys on your keyboard, or the W, A, S, D keys. The Enter key is used to choose a specific option once it has been highlighted.
  • You can also navigate using your mouse, hovering over options will highlight them, and left-clicking them will choose them.
  • To go back, you can use the escape (esc) key on your keyboard.
